# Privacy Policy — World's Camera Roll

_Last Updated: June 7, 2026_

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Dipper LLC (together with its
affiliates, "Company," "we," or "us") collects, uses, maintains, protects,
and discloses information from and about you when you download or use any
mobile application(s) published by the Company (the "Services"), including
World's Camera Roll ("WCR").

This Policy applies to information we collect in the Services and on any
other websites or apps where this Policy is posted. It does not apply to
information processed by third party artificial intelligence software that
is integrated into the Services.

By downloading or using the Services, you agree to allow us to collect
and process information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree
to this Policy, please do not use the Services.

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Collect It

We obtain information about you through the means discussed below when we
provide the Services. The type of information we collect depends on the
features you use on the Services. Please note that we need certain types
of information to provide the Services to you. If you do not provide us
with such information, you may no longer be able to access or use certain
Services features.

### Information You Provide Directly to Us

We may collect information when you use the Services. Information you may
provide us includes:

- **Account Information.** Your display name and any other information
  you provide us when creating an account. We use Apple's "Sign in with
  Apple" service to authenticate users; if you choose to share it, Apple
  may provide us with a private relay email address that forwards messages
  to your real email without disclosing it.
- **User Content.** Information about yourself that is included in
  videos, audio, photos, or other content you record or input into our
  Services ("Input"). The Services may produce composite videos, scene
  summaries, or other derived content based in part on the Input
  ("Output"). Output may or may not contain your personal information.
  Input and Output are collectively "Content".
- **Communication Information.** Information you provide when you
  interact with us (e.g., when you request information from us, interact
  with our personnel, create or modify an account with us, or provide us
  feedback).
- **Other Information.** Any other information you choose to share with
  us.

### Information We Collect from Minor Users

For users aged 13-17 ("Teen Users"), we limit data collection to what is
reasonably necessary for the core Services: account registration
information (display name, account identifier), Content the Teen User
creates or uploads, and basic device information (device type and
operating system). We do not collect precise geolocation, behavioral
tracking data, or contact lists from Teen Users. We delete Teen User
personal information within a reasonable time upon account deletion
request as required by applicable law.

### Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies

We may automatically collect information about your use of the Services,
including:

- **Device and Usage Information.** Device type, operating system,
  unique device identifiers (such as Apple's `identifierForVendor`), the
  type of device that you use, and mobile network information. We may
  also track when and how frequently you access or use the Services,
  including how you engage with or navigate our application.
- **Push Notification Tokens.** If you grant permission, we collect the
  Apple Push Notification token assigned to your device so that we can
  deliver notifications to you about activity in your sessions.
- **Sensor and Telemetry Data.** While you are recording, the Services
  collect motion sensor data, device orientation, camera settings, and
  related telemetry. This data is used to power features such as the
  multi-camera switcher and is recorded only while you are actively
  capturing.

### Location Information

We ask you to grant us access to your mobile device's geolocation data.
If you grant such permission, we may collect information about your
precise geolocation in order to discover existing capture sessions near
you or to start a new session at your current location. Location data
is collected only while the Services are in active use.

### Information From Third Party Sources

We may receive information about you from other Services users, such as
when they include you in Content they record (for example, a clip filmed
by another participant in a shared session may contain your image or
voice).

**Notice to Non-Users.** We may process your information if we receive
it from our users through Content recorded on the Services. This
information may include your image, voice, or other identifying
characteristics captured incidentally. We process this information even
if you are not a Services user and do not have an account with us. We
use this information solely to deliver the Services to the user who
recorded the Content.
